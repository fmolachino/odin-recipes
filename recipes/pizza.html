Some good Pizza

The famous "Some good Pizza"

This is the best pizza of the entire world

The recipe comes straight from Argentina

Ingredients:

  • Masa for Pizza
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 kilogram of mozarella
  • 300 grams of jam, good jam with low fat
  • 300 grams of Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Paint the masa with some tomato sauce
  2. Put it in the oven for 5 minutes
  3. While the masa is in the oven cut the cheese and reserve
  4. Took the masa out the oven and pour the chesse ontop of it
  5. Put the jam, and introduce it again in the oven
  6. Wait about 10 minutes and eat!
